COURSES & TRAINING

PADI Program

As an IDC Staff Instructor, Ricky is certified to teach the entire PADI program, from Open Water Diver to Divemaster, and provide Pre-Instructor Development Course (IDC) coaching.

Scuba Diving Referral Program

Students must complete the following steps to receive their PADI Open Water certification - 1) scuba diving theory; 2) pool skills; and 3) four open water dives. The referral program only focuses on the first two steps. Students must then take the referral and complete their training on their warm water vacation. Step 3 does not have to be decided at the time of training, but it must be completed within six months of the training class.

Includes all textbooks and all SCUBA equipment except for mask, snorkel, and fins (all students must supply their own mask, snorkel, and fins). The instructor will provide tanks, regulators, buoyancy compensators, weight belts, and wet suits. If students already own their own equipment, they are encouraged to use it in the pool.
